Pounded Yam

The African delicacy - Pounded Yam and Egusi soup

This a dish which when made using the pistel and mortar requires a lot of effort. But is about the most delicious meal you can have especially when the Egusi is well garnished with several condiments.

Ingredients
  • Yam
  • Blended Melon Seeds
  • Vegetables
  • Ponmo
  • Palm Oil
  • Blended Onions
  • Blended Pepper & Tomatoes
  • Salt
Steps
  1. Boil the yam for 30 mins
  2. While this is ongoing, get your mortar and pistel ready
  3. Put the hot yam in the mortar and pound with the pistel until all the lumps have dissappeared
  4. Put the pounded yam in a clean bowl
  5. Get a clean sizeable pot and heat it until it is dry
  6. Pour some palm oil in the pot and let it boil for about 5 minutes
  7. Pour the Blended Pepper mix into the sizzling oil and bring to boil for about 20 mins
  8. Once this is accomplished, begin moulding the egusi into balls and dropping them in the boiling soup
  9. Once done, cover and let it boil for another 45 minutes, remember to stir at intervals to avoid burning
  10. Add salt to taste
  11. Add the washed and chopped vegetable into the soup and let it simmer for another 20 minutes
  12. Your Egusi is done, enjoy it with the pounded yam
